The posterfeatures a sleek and professional design to convey the message "You Deserve the Highest Quality of Service." At the top, the tagline is displayed in bold, elegant font, creating an immediate impression of excellence and dedication.
The second poster highlights the role and responsibilities of a Human Resource Compensation and Benefits Officer, providing clear and attractive information to potential candidates about the position and how to apply.

The banners were created for the Kenya Institute of Bankers and Tribe for their upcoming events. For the Kenya Institute of Bankers, I designed a poster for the 2023 Interbank Football Tournament semi-final. The design features a dynamic image of a footballer in action, with a glowing football to highlight the excitement of the match.

For Tribe, I crafted a poster for the CEO Run at the Interbank Games Competition. The design showcases a close-up of running shoes in motion, emphasizing the theme of leadership and movement.
